# Code of Conduct

All contributors are expected to follow this code of conduct. Be respectful, constructive, and inclusive.

## Our Standards

- Be kind and professional.
- Respect different viewpoints and experiences.
- Provide constructive feedback; avoid personal attacks.
- Respect privacy and do not harass or discriminate.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Harassment, discrimination, abuse, or other harmful behavior will not be tolerated.

## Reporting

If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainers at conduct@your-org.example (replace with actual contact).

## Enforcement

Reports will be handled confidentially. Consequences may include warnings, temporary or permanent bans from contributing, or other actions as appropriate.
